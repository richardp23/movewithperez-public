# Photo shot list — example-realestate.dev

Specs for every shot: horizontal 3:2, >=3000px on the long edge, shot at golden hour or
bright overcast (no harsh noon shadows), no readable license plates, no identifiable
faces (no releases on file), hold the camera level. For the best scene in each set,
also grab a vertical. Phone is fine — wipe the lens, tap to expose for the building,
HDR on.

Map each delivered photo to a slot in docs/IMAGES.md and mark it Replaced.
